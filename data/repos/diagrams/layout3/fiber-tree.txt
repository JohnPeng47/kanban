FIBER TREE
==========
The internal shadow tree React maintains. Each node is a "Fiber" — a plain JS object.
Fibers link via .child (first child), .sibling (next sibling), .return (parent).
This is NOT a typical tree — it's a linked structure optimized for depth-first traversal.

Source: packages/react-reconciler/src/ReactFiber.js

TRAVERSAL ORDER (depth-first via the linked structure):
=======================================================

    performUnitOfWork walks this path:

    App          beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Header       beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Nav          beginWork -> no children -> completeWork
     |                                       then follow .sibling? no.
     |                                       follow .return to Header
     v                                       completeWork(Header)
    Header       completed, follow .sibling
     |
     v
    Board        beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Column[0]    beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Card[0]      beginWork -> leaf -> completeWork -> follow .sibling
     |
     v
    Card[1]      beginWork -> leaf -> completeWork -> no sibling
     |            follow .return to Column[0], completeWork
     v            follow .sibling to Column[1]
    Column[1]    beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Card[2]      leaf -> completeWork -> .return to Column[1]
     |            completeWork(Column[1]) -> .return to Board
     v            completeWork(Board) -> .return to App
    App          completeWork(App) -> done. Root complete.


THE ALTERNATE (double buffering):
=================================

    Every fiber has an .alternate pointing to its mirror.
    One is "current" (committed to DOM), the other is "workInProgress" (being built).
    After commit, they swap.

         current                    workInProgress
    +-------------+    .alternate    +-------------+
    |  <App />    | <--------------> |  <App />    |
    | memoized    |                  | pendingProps|
    | State: {...}|                  | (rendering) |
    +-------------+                  +-------------+

    On commit: root.current = workInProgress
    Next render: old current becomes the new workInProgress (recycled)
