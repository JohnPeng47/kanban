FIBER TREE TRAVERSAL ORDER
===========================
Depth-first traversal via the linked structure.
performUnitOfWork alternates between beginWork (descend) and completeWork (ascend).

Source: packages/react-reconciler/src/ReactFiberWorkLoop.js


    performUnitOfWork walks this path:

    App          beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Header       beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Nav          beginWork -> no children -> completeWork
     |                                       then follow .sibling? no.
     |                                       follow .return to Header
     v                                       completeWork(Header)
    Header       completed, follow .sibling
     |
     v
    Board        beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Column[0]    beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Card[0]      beginWork -> leaf -> completeWork -> follow .sibling
     |
     v
    Card[1]      beginWork -> leaf -> completeWork -> no sibling
     |            follow .return to Column[0], completeWork
     v            follow .sibling to Column[1]
    Column[1]    beginWork -> descend to child
     |
     v
    Card[2]      leaf -> completeWork -> .return to Column[1]
     |            completeWork(Column[1]) -> .return to Board
     v            completeWork(Board) -> .return to App
    App          completeWork(App) -> done. Root complete.
