COMMIT PHASE (summary)
========================
After render completes, apply all changes to the DOM.
Synchronous and not interruptible.

Source: packages/react-reconciler/src/ReactFiberCommitWork.js
        packages/react-reconciler/src/ReactFiberCommitEffects.js

See: commit-phases/ directory for detailed breakdown of each sub-phase.
